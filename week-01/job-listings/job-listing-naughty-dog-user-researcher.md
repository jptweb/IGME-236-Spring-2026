## The Role

Collaborate with developers to design and execute research activities including playtests, interviews, and surveys. Analyze findings, present insights to teams, and help embed user research into development pipelines.

- Design and execute research activities (playtests, interviews, surveys)
- Analyze findings and present insights to teams at various levels
- Help embed user research into development pipelines
- Mentor junior researchers
- Advocate for player perspectives throughout the organization
