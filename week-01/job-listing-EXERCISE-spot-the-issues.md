# UX Designer — FunTime Interactive

> **Note:** This is a fictional listing for classroom discussion. Can you spot what's wrong?

> **Location:** Remote
> **Salary:** $55,000 – $65,000/year

---

## About Us

FunTime Interactive is a fast-paced mobile game studio making hit casual games! We need a talented UX Designer to make our games look amazing!

---

## The Role

We're looking for a UX Designer to own the visual experience of our games. You'll create beautiful interfaces that wow our players and make our games stand out in the app store.

---

## Responsibilities

- Create pixel-perfect mockups and final visual assets in Figma and Photoshop
- Design icons, buttons, and UI elements that match our art style
- Make our menus and HUDs look polished and professional
- Work with our artists to ensure visual consistency across the game
- Deliver production-ready assets to our engineering team
- Keep up with the latest visual design trends
- Create promotional graphics for the app store

---

## Requirements

- 3+ years experience as a UX Designer
- Expert-level Figma and Adobe Creative Suite skills
- Strong graphic design portfolio with beautiful visual work
- Great eye for color, typography, and layout
- Ability to create icons and illustrations
- Passion for mobile games

---

## Bonus Points

- Motion design / After Effects experience
- 3D rendering skills (Blender, Cinema4D)
- Can create marketing materials
- Has worked on a shipped mobile game

---

## Why Join Us?

We're a small team with big dreams! You'll have creative freedom to make our games gorgeous. If you have amazing visual taste and want to make pretty things, apply now!
