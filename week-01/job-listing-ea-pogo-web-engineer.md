## About the Team

Pogo is a casual gaming platform operating since 1998, offering 50+ titles spanning popular genres like Solitaire, Mahjong, and Match 3. The studio focuses on high-quality HTML5-powered games with sophisticated metagames and social mechanics across desktop, tablet, and mobile.

---

## The Role

Develop and integrate new games on the Pogo platform, maintain existing games and internal tools like level editors.

---

## Key Responsibilities

- Develop and integrate new games on the Pogo platform
- Maintain existing games and internal tools like level editors
- Translate requirements into technical designs with risk assessment
- Write quality code and participate in code reviews
- Troubleshoot complex problems and coordinate with QA
- Research improvements to game and platform codebases

---

## Required Qualifications

- Bachelor's in Computer Science or equivalent experience
- 4+ years game development experience
- Proficiency in game mechanics, 3D spaces, physics, animation, and shaders
- Experience with game engines (Unity, Phaser, Cocos Creator)
- Strong understanding of design patterns, algorithms, and data structures
- Proficiency in **JavaScript/ES6+, HTML5**, Java, or .Net
- Game performance optimization expertise

---

## Preferred Qualifications

- **CSS3, Node, React, Angular** experience
- Version control (Git, Bitbucket)
- NoSQL databases (Elasticsearch, MongoDB, DynamoDB)
- Linux/Ubuntu experience
