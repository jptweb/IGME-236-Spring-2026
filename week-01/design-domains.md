# Design Domains: Untangling the Acronyms

---

## UX — User Experience

The **overall experience** of a person using a product—the entire spectrum of their journey, from start to finish.

> "User experience encompasses **all aspects** of the end-user's interaction with the company, its services, and its products." — Don Norman

---

## UI — User Interface

The **visual and tangible elements** the user actually touches and sees—buttons, screens, controls, colors, and layouts.

---

## HCI — Human-Computer Interaction

The **scientific study** of how humans interact with technology, using behavioral science and research to ensure systems are usable and safe.

---

## IA — Information Architecture

The **structural design of information environments**; organizing and labeling content so users can find their way.

---

## IxD — Interaction Design

The **design of the interaction flow** between users and products—the "conversation" that enables users to achieve their objectives.

---

## UCD — User-Centered Design

An **iterative design process** that brings in real users at every phase to refine the product based on their feedback and needs.

---
